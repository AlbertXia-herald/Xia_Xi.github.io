Evaluation Principle

Be explicit about the difference between reflection and evidence.

Evaluation pages are often the easiest place to over-claim. This structure separates confirmed findings from reflective commentary so the portfolio can remain credible even while some validation material is still being assembled.

Suggested additions

  • Accessibility observations
  • Feedback excerpts or screenshots
